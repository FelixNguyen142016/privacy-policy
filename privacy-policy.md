# Privacy Policy – Hydration & Stretch Reminder

Last updated: March 2026

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal 
user data. All settings (reminder intervals, sound preferences, quiet hours) 
are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API and 
are never sent to any external server.

This extension does not use cookies, analytics, or any third-party services.
